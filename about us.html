NATURE | ADVENTURE | TRAVEL

Leviticus Photography, a mobile photography brand founded on the 2nd of April 2014 by Sibusiso Mashinini, a young man from Soweto, South Africa.
Mobile photography, iPhoneography or smartphone photography simply means taking great pictures and creating works of art using a smartphone.

This group of artistic young minds started taking random pictures using their smartphones.
The whole idea was to capture the beauty of both Mother Nature & man-made moments and then share them with their families and friends.
Ever since they took their first pictures, Leviticus has been consistently aiming to produce quality photos that tell a story.
This concept is applied to ever picture that Leviticus produces.
