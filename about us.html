Young people who like to have fun, travel and taking pictures after the stresses of school and life are taken care of.
There’s no better way to unwind and let loose with good music and good friends, than with the youngsters of Leviticus,
a photography brand founded on the 2nd of April 2014 by Sibusiso Mashinini, a young man from Soweto, South Africa.

Leviticus is a Late Latin word (the thrid book of the Old Testament, containing the laws relating to priests and Levites).
This group of artistic young minds started taking random pictures using their smartphones.
The whole idea was to capture the beauty of both mother nature & man-made moments and then share them with their friends and families.
Ever since they took their first pictures, Leviticus has been consistently aiming to produce quality photos that tell a story.
This concept is applied to each and ever picture that Leviticus produces.
